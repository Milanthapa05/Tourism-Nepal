Why Nepal ?

Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, famous for Mount Everest, rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and breathtaking landscapes. It offers unforgettable experiences for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and cultural explorers. Nepal is a land of peace with most welcoming peoples, place where tourists are considered as guests and guests are considered as avataar of god.
